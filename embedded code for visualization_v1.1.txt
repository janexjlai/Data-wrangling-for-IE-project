

# visualization: obesity in australia
Overall overweight and obesity is observing a constantly growing trend among Australia. According to Australian Institute of Health and Welfare survey results, 34.7% of boys aged 10-13 are keeping excessive body weight in 2014, figure, however, in 1995 survey for the same age group is 23.3%. Meanwhile, figure for girls aging between 14 and 17 are increasing nearly twice from 14.8% (1995) to 27.7% (2014).

# visualization: number and proportion of kids who meet physical activity standards
Statistics show that the proportion of kids who can meet physical activity standard for kids are decreasing by growing age. Around 35.7% of children from 5 to 8 years old stated that they met the standards for seven days prior to the survey, while only 5.8% kids above age 15 said they met the recommendations.
